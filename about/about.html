Rock-el Ethiopia is

A charitable and development indigenous non-governmental organization officially established being registered under the Federal Democratic Republic of Ethiopia Civil Societies Organizations (CSOs) Authority with the proclamation number 1113/21 and a registration number 6490 by October 22, 2022. It is a board-led organization and has been endowed all the necessary documents like certificate and manual.
Rock-el Ethiopia wants to address the immediate needs of the people in vulnerability like food, medication, clothe, education material and others in a way that does not create dependency attitude that the targeted beneficiaries will engage themselves into self-helping life status through proving the necessary technical and financial support as sooner as possible.
Besides, Rock-el Ethiopia engages in various development programs such as education, health, environmental protection and economic empowerment activities to contribute to the overall Ethiopia government development programs.

Rock-el Ethiopia mission

Providing the targeted beneficiaries with the necessary technical, material and financial support so that they are able to maximize their potential for productivity

Rock-el Ethiopia Vision

Seeing people economically stable and equitably served (accessed) so that they become productive and selfreliant .

Main Objectives

➖ To access equitable and inclusive education to the students with disabilities by providing the necessary facilities and devoted teachers.
➖ To create conducive work environment and providing relevant vocational trainings for the unemployed & under employed youth in order to enable them engaged into appropriate business.


➖ To organize, train and mature poor women technically with basic business skills and access them to finance to enable them join small business in their context.
➖To create better awareness and capacity of urban & semi-urban community to keep their surrounding clean and fulfilling the sanitary facilities like common toilet, shower, waste disposal garbage.

Program Description and Rationales

Rock-el Ethiopia has three program components: humanitarian service, development and advocacy .

Charity Program
focuses on supporting the people in urgent basic needs (nutrition, clothing, shelter, education, health, etc.) with the modality of ABCD (Asset Based Community Development) Approach in which the beneficiaries will get preliminary services and passes to self-helping state through maximizing their potential through technical, material and financial supports. Rock-el Ethiopia boldly shares the big saying of our world Mother Theresa “I am the tip of a small pencil in the hands of God that He uses to write a love letter for human beings He created in His image.” Humanity is the central element of the organization because human beings are the most valuable assets of our world. Rock-el aspires to raise people in urgent needs of helps of the all age groups.
Development Program
emphases on quality contribution for equitability and sustainable development. This program wing will contribute to the government program on the top burning issues that will affect the development of the country such as enhancing inclusive education, youth economic empowerment that will save the young generation from addiction & illegal migration, responding to the effects of growing global climatic change through environmental conservation programs and also delivering WASH program for the fast growing urbanization mainly in relation with waste management and accessing clean water in urban and semi-urban areas.
Advocacy Program
in the developed countries, Civil Society Organizations (CSOs) are responsible to play a vital role in awakening community to ask for their rights; to challenge the government officials for their failures and to utter the voice of those under any political, economic and social problems. However, in our country, the government used to oppress CSOs not to intervene in any advocacy sectors including human right issues for the last couple of decades. Despite of this fact, the current government of Ethiopia (under reform) has begun to revive the CSOs active involvement in the areas of advocacy mainly in developing social accountability. Therefore, Rock-el Ethiopia has come up with the interest of contributing what it can to the areas like inclusiveness (effective participation of all groups in any socio-economic affairs), human right issues and social accountability experience.
Rock-el Ethiopia Principles
➖Integration and maintaining the interest of community under target, the regulation & proclamation of the local government and the interest of the donors.
➖ Consortium is the word of the day; integration is the way to sooner impacting. Thus, partnership is power!
➖“Sustainability” should be „the end‟ we should begin with „in mind‟ often while designing a project.

Goal

Contribute to the resource optimization for the sustainable socio-economic development through effective training and efficient use of available resources.

Method and Approach

Community Based Intervention

that realizes the direct involvement of community, public institutions, stake holders, CBOs, FBOs, and private business owners for better impacting and ensuring sustainability of each programs.

Asset Based Community Development (ABCD)

in which the community grows a sense of ownership and can easily realize sustainability of the programs or projects.

Partnership and Volunteers Engagement:

to realize the programs integration and sooners impact in changing the lives of the targeted people and make the implementations cost-effective and time efficient
